Why Hamburg?

The city of bridges.

Hamburg has over 2,500 bridges, the most of any city in the world. That's more than Amsterdam, Venice, and London combined. Bridges you need to visit when in Hamburg: Köhlbrandbrücke, Überseebrücke, Brooksbrücke, Poggenmühlenbrücke, and Feenteichbrücke.

Cafés

My favorite Cafés in Hamburg

Codos Coffee

The Codos has one mission: to make really good coffee without letting the do's and don'ts of coffee fanatics take the fun out of it.

Address

Bahrenfelder Strasse 137-139, 22765

What i like about it

This is one of the best cafes in Hamburg.In the middle of the room is your own coffee roasting machine. So you really can't get the coffee any fresher!

Herr Max

Herr Max not only has what is perhaps the best cake in Hamburg, but also the most beautiful: in the shop window you can see unusual cake creations for every occasion.Herr Max's team also bakes a large part of the range vegan

Address

Schulterblatt 12 20357

What i like about it

There is something to cater for everyone and everything is homemade.

Nord Coast Coffee Roastery

Whether it's for work or a lazy Sunday afternoon, the Nord Coast Coffee Roastery on Nikolaifleet is always a good choice. If you're lucky, you'll get one of the popular window seats with a direct view of the canal.

Address

Deichstraße 9 20459

What i like about it

You can watch coffee beans getting roasted while you are waiting on your order.
